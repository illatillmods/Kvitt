# Product

## Problem

Receipts are rich but messy. Users know what they spent in total, but not what they actually bought across stores, brands, and categories.

## MVP goal

Turn a receipt image into a clean, readable item list with lightweight categorization and enough structure to support later analytics.

## MVP principles

- bias toward fast feedback over full automation
- keep OCR and normalization replaceable
- ship useful item-level output before deeper analytics

## Primary user flow

1. open the app
2. confirm backend connectivity
3. load a demo or scan a receipt
4. review normalized items and totals
